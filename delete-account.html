Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your FitMonster account using either of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the FitMonster app and sign in to your account
  2. Tap the profile icon or navigate to Edit Profile
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

support@fitmonster.com

Please include the email address associated with your FitMonster account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data TypeActionTimeframe
Account credentialsPermanently deletedImmediately
Profile information (name, age, gender, etc.)Permanently deletedImmediately
Monster collection and progressPermanently deletedImmediately
Workout history and exercise logsPermanently deletedImmediately
Food scan and nutrition logsPermanently deletedImmediately
Battle history and recordsPermanently deletedImmediately
Friend connections and chat messagesPermanently deletedImmediately
Daily stats and quest progressPermanently deletedImmediately
Push notification tokensPermanently deletedImmediately

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your monsters, workout history, food logs, and social connections will be lost forever. Please make sure you want to proceed before deleting your account.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

All personal data is deleted immediately upon confirmation. We do not retain any user data after account deletion. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to your account may be retained for service improvement purposes.
